Side Catcher

Feature Specification Image FAQs Video

  • Entire System is "Quick-on Quick-off"

  • Tilt to dump pull rope provides the operator with a quick dump option without leaving their position

  • Catcher is light weight, 1/4th the weight of metal catchers

  • Metal handles to lift off and dump provide easy handling and dumping

  • Angled pan reduces potential damage from struck objects

  • Discharge dust hood directs dust away from the operator

    • Zero turn mowers discharge to rear

    • Walk behind mowers discharge to ground

  • System capacity is either 3.25, 3.75, or 4.3 Bu. depending on mower
